1. Contoh Prompt

Primary Video Purpose: Film Teaser / Trailer
Aspect Ratio: 16:9 (Horizontal)
Visual Style: Realistic, with a dark (gritty) cinematic feel
Lighting: Dramatic lighting from city neon lights at night
Background/Setting: Narrow alley between tall buildings, wet asphalt reflecting neon light

Storyline/Sequence of Scenes:
1. (0-2 seconds) Eye Level angle on the Protagonist character (child). He stands alone, staring at a puddle on the ground with a curious expression.
2. (2-4 seconds) The camera performs a very slow motion movement. From the reflection in the puddle, we see the silhouette of the Antagonist (teenager) slowly appearing behind the child.
3. (4-5 seconds) Extreme close-up on the Protagonist's eyes widening in surprise as he realizes the Antagonist's presence. At this exact moment, lightning strikes in the sky, briefly illuminating his face.

Dialog: No dialog.
Narasi / Voice-over (Bahasa Indonesia): "Dia di sini."
Audio Elements: Background Music - Emotional and suspenseful

Apa itu Prompt Maker?

Prompt Maker adalah sebuah alat berbasis web yang membantu Anda menyusun deskripsi teks (prompt) yang detail dan terstruktur untuk digunakan pada platform generator video berbasis AI seperti Runway, Pika, atau Sora.

Apa beda Satu Kali Pembelian dengan Langganan?

Satu Kali Pembelian berarti Anda hanya membayar sekali untuk mendapatkan akses selamanya ke fitur-fitur dalam paket tersebut. Langganan adalah model pembayaran berulang (misalnya bulanan) untuk mempertahankan akses.
